The Greatest Playwright in History

William Shakespeare shaped literature with stories of love and loss, ambition and betrayal, kings and fools. His words have survived centuries, echoing through theatres, classrooms, films, and culture worldwide.

Shakespeare’s Life & Legacy

Early Life

Born in Stratford-upon-Avon in 1564, Shakespeare grew up in a modest but influential family.

London Years

In London, Shakespeare became a playwright and actor for the Lord Chamberlain’s Men.

Later Works

His later plays explored ambition, madness, love, and betrayal with unmatched depth.

Legacy

Shakespeare’s influence continues across literature, theater, film, and modern language.
