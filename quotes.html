Shakespeare's Works


Shakespeare has a vast collection of works, with up to 190 published writings in his lifetime. He has about 38 plays and 154 sonnets. Fun fact: Universities offer specialized programs for Shakespeare alone, studying his life, works, and analyzing each text! Almost, if not all, of his plays are translated into every major language here on Earth, showcasing his true global impact and legacy.


Here is a collection and summarization of some of his most famous works. From tragedies to comedies to histories to sonnets, we provide a short summary/analysis of his texts as well as some additional resources you can use for more learning.
